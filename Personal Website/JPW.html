Funner to be a Runner!

Many people ask me why I run. The quick answer is to get in shape. Running will get you in physical shape no doubt, but this activity goes beyond being an exercise for your body. It's an exercise for the mind. People don't run because they fear it. Fear of looking sluggish, not finishing, or enduring pain. Believe me, every runner has had those feeling before, but we look beyond the temporary. Looking back makes us lose energy so we continue forward and set goals to reach a destination. This is the art of running.

"If it doesn't challenge you, it won't change you".
